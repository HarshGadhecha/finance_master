Privacy Policy for Finance Master App

At Finance Master, we take your privacy seriously. 
We understand that the data you share with us is personal and sensitive, and we want to assure you that we are committed to protecting it. 
This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data that you provide to us while using our app.

Data Collection :

Finance Master does not collect any personal data or sensitive information from you. 
We do not require you to provide us with any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or financial details. 
All calculations are performed locally on your device without any external communication. 
We do not use any third-party services or software to collect data from your device.

Data Usage :

All calculations made using Finance Master are only for educational purposes. 
We do not collect or store any data that is generated by the app. 
We do not have access to any information that you input into the app, including the interest rates, payment amounts, or other financial information. 
All data is kept securely on your device and is not shared with us or any other third party.

Data Protection :

Finance Master is designed to operate entirely on your device. 
We do not store any information on our servers, and we do not transmit data over the internet. 
We take the security of your information seriously and use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect 
your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. 
We also regularly review and update our security measures to ensure that your data is always protected.
